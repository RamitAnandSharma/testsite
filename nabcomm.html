WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

One house - one app


Overwhelmed with too many hubs, apps and remote controls? Nabcomm makes it easy to manage most devices in your house from one app and through one gateway; wireless cameras, lighting, temperature and remote controls. Using the Nabcomm SmartHome App, you can enter time specific settings to lower your energy consumption without compromising comfort. With the Nabcomm SmartHome solution you are able to control everything from everywhere and at any time. Welcome to your Smart Home â€“ a Smart way of living!

ENERGY SAVING

Enjoy energy cost savings of up to 40%


The Nabcomm SmartHome App and smart solutions are made to monitor and reduce energy consumption without compromising comfort. Connecting electrical heaters to the HomeControl smart outlet has proved to give more than 30% energy reduction per heater. The system takes 14 days to learn your temperature preferences. Any change in the outside temperature will be captured and the necessary adjustments made in order to maintain your selected comfort or saving mode temperatures. You may also monitor the temperature at your cabin from the same App.

EASY AND CONVENIENT

Dim the lights with your smartphone


It has never been easier to install an advanced lighting system. You can easily replace your existing lighting sources with Any smart light bulbs, Phillips HUE or Osram Lightify. Then you can dim the lights immediately, over a time period, or according to scenarios you have predefined in the App. You can also set your outdoor lights to switch on at sunset and off at sunrise. You now have a smart and advanced lighting system - all Do-It-Yourself.

SECURITY

Follow every motion in your house


Register the wireless camera in the HomeControl App and you can monitor any irregular motion in your home or at the office from wherever you are. In the video recording you will be able to see what happened 10 seconds before the sensor was triggered.

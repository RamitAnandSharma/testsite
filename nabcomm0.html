One house - one app

Overwhelmed with too many hubs, apps and remote controls? HomeControl makes it easy to manage most devices in your house from one app and through one gateway; wireless cameras, lighting, temperature and remote controls for TV or music. Using the patented HomeControl outlet, you can enter time specific temperature levels in the HomeControl App and lower your energy consumption without compromising comfort. With the HomeControl solution you are able to control everything from everywhere and at any time. Welcome to your Smart Home â€“ a Smart way of living!

Enjoy energy cost savings of up to 30%

The HomeControl App and smart solutions are made to monitor and reduce energy consumption without compromising comfort. Connecting electrical heaters to the HomeControl smart outlet has proved to give more than 30% energy reduction per heater. The system takes 14 days to learn your temperature preferences. Any change in the outside temperature will be captured and the necessary adjustments made in order to maintain your selected comfort or saving mode temperatures. You may also monitor the temperature at your cabin from the same App.

Dim the lights with your smartphone

It has never been easier to install an advanced lighting system. You can easily replace your existing lighting sources with HomeControl smart light bulbs, Phillips HUE or Osram Lightify. Then you can dim the lights immediately, over a time period, or according to scenarios you have predefined in the App. You can also set your outdoor lights to switch on at sunset and off at sunrise. You now have a smart and advanced lighting system - all Do-It-Yourself.

Follow every motion in your house

Register the wireless camera in the HomeControl App and you can monitor any irregular motion in your home or at the office from wherever you are. The camera is ranked one of the best in the market in terms of picture quality, and it has one of the most secure cloud solutions available. Advanced technology in the camera makes it possible to discover both sound and motion and send you email warnings immediately. In the video recording you will be able to see what happened 10 seconds before the sensor was triggered.

What is Bootstrap?


Bootstrap is a free collection of tools for creating websites and web applications. It contains HTML and CSS-based design templates for typography, forms, buttons, navigation and other interface components, as well as optional JavaScript extensions. It is the No.1 project on GitHub with 65,000+ stars and 23,800 forks (as of March 2014) [1] and has been used by NASA and MSNBC, among many others..
